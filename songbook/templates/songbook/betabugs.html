Notice: Site Under Development

Welcome to my application! Please note that the site is still under active development, and there are a few known bugs that I’m currently working to resolve.

The good news is that these issues are minor and are unlikely to affect your experience on the site. However, if you do encounter anything unusual, I’d love to hear from you! Your feedback is incredibly valuable and helps me improve.

Latest improvements

  • 2025-02-17: Added Posted/Updated column Any song Posted/Updated prior to January are part of a batch import and have not been updated to take advantage of StrumSphere formatting.
  • 2025-02-17: Chord diagram for 2 instruments Are you running a struming club? You can prepare PDF with the chord diagrams for two differents instruments! Simply set your preference under your profile!
  • 2025-02-14: Chord transposition: User can transpose up or down up to 7 semitones without changing the key of the original score.
  • 2025-02-13: Song formatting You can change many visual aspect of the song to enhanced your PDF files.

Upcoming Improvements:

  • Leader Permissions: Grant admin access for teachers, trainers, and club leaders.
  • Sharing formatted song: Let other users use your embellished score.
  • Additional Chord Diagrams: Adding different variations for each chord.
  • Sheet Music Verification: Checking key, year, first note, composer, and comparing with YouTube clips.
  • Improved Upload Tool: Enhancing the process of adding new sheet music.
  • Better Tagging System: Improving song categorization with Taggit.
  • Title page for multipage reportAdding title page for multipage report with Table of content and event related information

Thank you for your patience and support as I continue refining the site. If you’d like to report an issue, please email me at gaulindm@gmail.com.
