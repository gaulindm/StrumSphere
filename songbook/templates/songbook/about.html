Welcome to StrumSphere

Your ultimate online songbook designed for ukulele clubs and strummers of all kinds! Whether you play ukulele, baritone ukulele, banjo, or guitar—including left-handed versions—we've got you covered.

What You'll Find on StrumSphere:

  • A growing collection of songs with easy-to-read chord diagrams.
  • Support for multiple instruments, so everyone can play along.
  • Clear, bolded chords to keep you in sync while strumming.
  • A fun and engaging space for musicians of all skill levels.

No matter what you play or how you strum, StrumSphere is here to bring music lovers together. Stay tuned and get ready to strum along with us!

StrumSphere wants to thank Heather J. Topps of the North Bay Ukulele Club for her well tought out scores well prepared PDF layout.

Let’s make music, together!
